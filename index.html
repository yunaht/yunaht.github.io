5 Fun facts about Cats you didn't know about

Read time approx. 5mins

Cats can be right-pawed or left-pawed, similar to human handedness.

Cats can make over 100 different sounds, while dogs make only about 10.

Cats rub their faces on objects to mark their territory with scent glands on their cheeks.

Cats sweat through the pads on their paws, unlike humans who sweat all over.

The oldest known pet cat was found in a 9,500-year-old grave on the island of Cyprus.
